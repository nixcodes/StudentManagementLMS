Documentation,

This project has three main interface general homepage which is common for everyone and then students login page and teacher login page.

Home Page,

This page act as the index of project people is redirected here when they enter the url. Then from this page they can navigate to About, Documentation, and Login page. Then they can login as student or as teacher depending upon the role they are assigned with.

Teacher Login,

The login credentials of the teacher is created by the developer itself. When they login as a teacher then they can have the following options like, creating students, viewing the students, updating marks to the students, sending them mails regarding assignments, important remainders or their newly updated marks, receiving feedback of the students.

student Login,

The student login id and password is provided by the Teacher and they can login only if their marks are updated for the subject else they can't. As a student you can view you marks and other students marks of their class. They can also send feedback to their teacher or an message regarding their teaching and questions.
